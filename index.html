BAUSE Enterprise LLC — Professional Scope

BAUSE Enterprise LLC is a Black-Owned, Veteran-Owned, Woman-Led Business delivering professional services with a commitment to excellence, accountability, and results. Services include contracting support, consulting, project management, cleaning and property services, property management support, and administrative services.

  • Services performed according to agreed requirements and schedules
  • Professional standards and clear communication throughout delivery
  • Documentation and reporting available upon request

Typical Deliverables

  • Pre-service intake (needs, schedule, access, expectations)
  • Service execution and/or coordination with quality control
  • Completion confirmation (walk-through as applicable)
  • Electronic invoicing and documentation as needed

Exclusions (Unless Added)

  • Hazardous material handling
  • Licensed trade work unless via approved vendors
  • Emergency services without prior agreement
