BAUSE Enterprise LLC — Professional Scope

BAUSE Enterprise LLC is a Black-owned, ambitious woman-led business delivering high-quality professional services with a focus on excellence, integrity, and results. Services include cleaning, property support, logistics/dispatch administrative assistance, and operational administrative support for individuals and organizations.

  • Provide services according to client requirements and agreed schedules
  • Maintain professional standards and clear communication throughout delivery
  • Use checklists, documentation, and reporting when requested

What’s Included (Typical)

  • Pre-service intake (needs, schedule, access, expectations)
  • Service execution with quality control
  • Completion confirmation and client walk-through (as applicable)
  • Invoices and documentation provided electronically

What’s Not Included (Unless Added)

  • Hazardous material handling
  • Licensed trade work (electrical/plumbing) unless via approved vendors
  • Unscheduled emergency services without agreement
